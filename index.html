Eyebrow design

Eyebrow design is a meticulous process that involves shaping and sculpting the eyebrows to create a well-defined and symmetrical appearance.

It takes into account the individual's facial features, bone structure, and personal preferences to create an eyebrow shape that enhances their natural beauty.

Through techniques such as waxing, tweezing, and trimming, eyebrow design can transform the face by framing the eyes and providing a polished and sophisticated look.

per session

Eyebrow design and coloring

Eyebrow design and coloring is a specialized service aimed at shaping and enhancing the eyebrows to achieve a desired look that complements the individual's features.

It involves expertly shaping the eyebrows to create a flattering arch and filling in any sparse areas with a suitable color to achieve a defined and polished appearance.

This process can transform the face by framing the eyes and providing a harmonious balance to the overall facial aesthetic

450 ₴

per session

Spa henna eyelash tinting

Spa henna eyelash tinting is a popular beauty treatment that involves applying henna dye to the lashes to create a temporary, natural-looking tint.

This procedure is gentle and safe, making it suitable for those with sensitive eyes.

The henna dye not only adds depth and color to the lashes but also nourishes and conditions them, resulting in a fuller and more luscious appearance.

450 ₴

per session

Facial threading

Facial threading is a precise and ancient hair removal technique that originated in the Middle East and India.

It involves using a twisted thread to remove unwanted hair from the face, including the eyebrows, upper lip, and chin.

This method is highly effective in shaping the eyebrows, achieving clean facial contours, and providing long-lasting results without the use of chemicals or wax.

350 ₴

per session

Facial waxing

Facial waxing is a popular method of hair removal that involves the application of warm wax to the facial area and then swiftly removing it, along with the unwanted hair, using a cloth strip.

This technique is commonly used to remove hair from the eyebrows, upper lip, chin, and cheeks.

Facial waxing provides smooth and long-lasting results, leaving the skin hair-free and giving a clean and polished appearance.

300 ₴

per session

Eyelash lamination

Eyelash lamination is a popular beauty treatment that enhances the appearance of natural eyelashes.

It involves applying a specialized solution to lift, curl, and set the lashes in place, creating a fuller and more defined look.

The process typically takes about one hour and the results can last for several weeks, giving you a low-maintenance option for beautiful lashes.

500 ₴

per session

Eyelash extensions

Eyelash extensions are a semi-permanent beauty treatment that involves attaching individual synthetic lashes to your natural lashes, enhancing their length, thickness, and curl.

The process is meticulously done by a trained professional, and the results can last for several weeks with proper care.

Eyelash extensions provide a dramatic and glamorous look, eliminating the need for mascara and giving you effortlessly stunning lashes.

450 ₴

per session

Eyelash botox

Eyelash botox, also known as lash botox or lash lift, is a cosmetic treatment that aims to enhance the appearance of eyelashes by giving them a lifted and curled look.

The procedure involves the use of a specialized solution that is applied to the lashes, helping to relax and soften them for a defined and fuller appearance.

Eyelash botox can last for several weeks, providing a low-maintenance option for those seeking to achieve more voluminous and beautiful lashes.

400 ₴

per session
