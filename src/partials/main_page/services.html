Facial threading

Facial threading is a precise and ancient hair removal technique that originated in the Middle East and India.

It involves using a twisted thread to remove unwanted hair from the face, including the eyebrows, upper lip, and chin.

This method is highly effective in shaping the eyebrows, achieving clean facial contours, and providing long-lasting results without the use of chemicals or wax.

Facial waxing

Facial waxing is a popular method of hair removal that involves the application of warm wax to the facial area and then swiftly removing it, along with the unwanted hair, using a cloth strip.

This technique is commonly used to remove hair from the eyebrows, upper lip, chin, and cheeks.

Facial waxing provides smooth and long-lasting results, leaving the skin hair-free and giving a clean and polished appearance.
