Strict rules

We love our work, so we take it very seriously and adhere to strict rules.

  • 1

    Cleanliness

    and hygiene in all areas of the beauty salon, including regular cleaning and disinfection of workstations, tools, and equipment.

  • 2

    Adherence

    to all sanitary norms and regulations, including the use of disposable materials and hygiene products when necessary

  • 3

    Development

    of the staff, providing them with opportunities for training and qualification enhancement.

  • 4

    Inspection

    of quality and control of the products used, prohibition of using uncertified cosmetic products.

  • 5

    Safety

    clear procedures to protect both clients and staff, including providing instructions for the operation of equipment and the use of cosmetic products.

  • 6

    Compliance

    of the services offered by the beauty salon to quality and safety standards.

  • 7

    Prohibition

    of performing services if the client has contraindications or a health condition that prevents the procedure from being carried out.

  • 8

    Protection

    and confidentiality of clients' personal data, compliance with relevant information protection regulations.
